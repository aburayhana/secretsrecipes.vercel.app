##### The ingredients needed to make Honey Chilli Chicken Wings:

1. Make ready 4 full chicken wings
1. Get  Water
1. Take 100 g margarine melted
1. Take 1/4 tsp baking powder
1. Take 2 tblsp soy sauce
1. Make ready 1 tblsp honey
1. Take 1 tblsp hot sauce
1. Make ready 2 tblsp sriracha




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Honey Chilli Chicken Wings:

1. Preheat oven on bake to 230 degrees. In a sauce pan. Place chicken wings in enough water to cover them and bring to boil. Boil for 3 minutes and set aside.
1. In a separate bowl melt margarine, and add baking powder, soy sauce, honey and hot sauce and sriracha and mix (I forgot the sriracha so I added it just in time) Pour in chicken and stir. Bring to a boil then transfer to baking dish and bake for 15 minutes.
1. Reduce temperature to 190 degrees and glacé and turn wings. You’ll do this for every 15 minutes. Till reduced and sticky. (In this moment I cut the outer outer wing because it burns easily. You can do this in the very beginning) garnish with sesame seeds. Serve immediately.
